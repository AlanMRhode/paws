About Adoption

To view our adoptable pets, click here. When you adopt a new best friend from PAWS, you are saving a life that might not have had a chance without your kindness. The first step is to fill out our adoption application, which is available here or at any of our adoption locations.

Applications generally take 24-48 hours for approval. There is a possibility for same day adoption, but we cannot guarantee it. Please present your application at a PAWS Adoption Center in person at least one hour prior to closing time. You may also mail it to 100 N. 2nd Street, Philadelphia, PA 19106 or email it to adoptions@phillypaws.org.
